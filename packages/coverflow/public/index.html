Portfolio Flywheel Effect

Shared Creative Resources

Internal writers and producers Jack Schrepferman (Kobalt/100 Publishing) and Leon Sharplin (Berklee graduate, multiple publishing offers) already work across four roster artists. A single hit places multiple names—and therefore multiple royalty streams—on the same master recording.

Hudson's support runs place him in front of ~200,000 aggregate ticket-buyers over two tours. Historical Live Nation data shows 8% of an opener's nightly audience becomes monthly listeners within 30 days. That implies ~16,000 new listeners for every roster mate who appears on Hudson's setlist.
