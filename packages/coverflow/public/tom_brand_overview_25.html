who he is

poet heart. farm kid hands. best-friend warmth.

Tom Siletto writes like a poet, lives like a farm kid, and carries the warmth of a best friend you grew up with. Raised on the Northern California coast between surf breaks and family farmland, his music blends folk-pop intimacy with lyrical honesty. It’s Big Sur in a rainstorm — cinematic, nostalgic, and always with room for a laugh.

the visual world

The Tom Siletto visual palette is organic, textural, and cinematic — greens, seafoam blues, warm earth tones. Film grain, wool coats, wet stone, corduroy. Photography should feel lived-in and timeless, as if pulled from a box of perfectly aged prints.
